Let's go to visit Saudi Arabia

Tabuk City

The city of Tabuk, the seat of the Emirate of the Tabuk region and the largest city in northern Saudi Arabia, and around it are some of the most important antiquities in the Arabian Peninsula. The Tabuk region is the northern gateway to the Arabian Peninsula, and a vital route for trade, pilgrims and Umrah performers from outside the Arabian Peninsula. It is one of the important agricultural areas in the Kingdom.

Alola City

Al-Ula is one of the cities of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, located in the northwest of the Kingdom, and administratively affiliated to the Emirate of Al-Madinah Al-Munawwarah Region, approximately 300 km north from the center of the Emirate, and the capital of Al-Ula Governorate. Human settlement is about 4,000 years old, and it is one of the most important tourist destinations in Saudi Arabia.
